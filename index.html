Techno India Journal on Strategic Business Mind (TIJSBM)

TIJSBM is a peer-reviewed international journal committed to advancing research in strategic management, business analytics, and interdisciplinary business studies. The journal provides a scholarly platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to publish high-quality original research, review papers, and case studies.

The journal promotes innovative thinking in business strategy, organizational leadership, finance, marketing, and emerging management domains, fostering global academic collaboration and knowledge dissemination. TIJSBM encourages evidence-based research that contributes to both theoretical development and practical managerial implications in the rapidly evolving business environment.

Focus Areas & Submission Guidelines

Focus Areas :

TIJSBM invites high-quality submissions in, but not limited to, the following areas:

  • Strategic Management, Marketing & Consumer Behaviour, Finance & Accounting, Human Resource Management, Entrepreneurship & Innovation, Business Analytics, Digital Transformation, and related domains in Business and Management.
  • Research addressing emerging trends in the global business environment, including data-driven decision making, financial technologies (FinTech), sustainability, and organizational excellence.
  • Cutting-edge theoretical, empirical, and applied research that advances strategic thinking and managerial practices across industries.
Submission Guidelines :
  • All submissions must strictly adhere to TIJSBM author guidelines.
  • Manuscripts should present original, unpublished work not under review elsewhere.
  • Papers should demonstrate significant academic contribution and practical relevance in the field of Business and Management.
